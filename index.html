Raspberry Pi Doorbell

The Raspberry Pi doorbell was a fun little side project I did. The doorbell in our house is too far away for me to hear it when I am in my room, so I thought it would be cool to get a notification on my smartphone when someone was at the door. The hardware was done with some very simple soldering and a couple of wires. For the software I used Python.

Xencoder

Xencoder is another side project of mine. I needed a simple application to convert video files into a format that could be used for Youtube, but I could not find one that was simple enough for my needs, so I made one. The application itself is written in C#. It uses the FFmpeg framework for the encoding of the video.
